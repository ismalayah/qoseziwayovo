Hazel Sanchez Biography-Wiki

Hazel Sanchez is an American News anchor and reporter. She joined CBS 2 in 2000 as a general assignment reporter. Prior to joing to WCBS-TV, Sanchez was the weekend anchor/reporter for WBAY-TV Green Bay, WI.

She also hosted and moderated “Teen Connection,” alive, hour-long show for Wisconsin Public Television in Green Bay during that time. She also hosted a number of the station’s award-winning documentaries at “Teen Connection,”.

Before working in Green Bay, she was a news cut-in anchor and assistant producer at WISN-TV in Milwaukee. Sanchez is best known for earning a Midwest Emmy award, a CINE Golden Eagle award, and an award from the Wisconsin Broadcast Association.

Also, she received awards for hosting “The Discovery of Dawn,” an emotional documentary on one young woman’s battle with Anorexia Nervosa.
