Mish Michaels is listed on the Wikipedia official page. Mish is a broadcast meteorologist, environmental reporter, and author from the United States.

She was born in Kolkata, India, and graduated from Cornell University in Ithaca, New York, with a Bachelor of Science in Meteorology and Harvard University with a Master of Science in Technology.

Her first work after college was with WMUR in Manchester, New Hampshire, as a weekend meteorologist/environmental reporter.

Mish received a Metcalf Fellowship from the Metcalf Institute for Marine and Environmental Reporting in 2006.

She began working at WBZ-TV in September 2001 and left in July 2009. She spent her time after leaving WBZ raising her children and authoring books in her leisure time.

Michaels announced her appointment with WGBH as a science reporter on Twitter on January 31, 2017.
