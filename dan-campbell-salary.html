The manager of the National Football League’s Detroit Lions is Dan Campbell, a former tight end and current coach (NFL).
Dan Campbell served as the Miami Dolphins interim head coach and tight ends coach, as well as the New Orleans Saints from 2016 to 2020 when he also served in the same capacities as associate head coach.
From 2010 to 2015, Campbell was an assistant coach for the Miami Dolphins.
Campbell played football for Texas A”AND”M in college. In the 1999 NFL Draft, the New York Giants selected Dan Campbell in the third round. Campbell later played for the Dallas Cowboys, the Detroit Lions, and the New Orleans Saints. As a player, Campbell helped the Giants make it to the Super Bowl in 2000.
