Agron started dancing at the early age of three and began to teach dance in her early teens. She was not very popular during her school days although she had many friends who liked her company.

Agron has acquired her wealth from the many TV shows and small film appearances. She has performed in many TV shows like CSI: NY, Shark, Numb3rs, and Close to Home. She has kept on having recurring roles in Veronica Mars. In the short film, It’s a Mall World, Agron appeared as Harper and also in the second season of the TV series Heroes as the head cheerleader. She has also been one of the hosts to the music festival named Chickens in Love. Agron was among the many Hollywood stars, who were the face of the Wal-Mart’s Ocean Pacific’s marketing campaign. This campaign was started in all the leading fashion magazines of the US like the Vogue, Cosmopolitan, and Elle. Her most notable role till date which has made her a popular face has been the character of Quinn Fabray as a cheerleader for Glee.

She has always maintained privacy about her personal life. In an interview for the Cosmopolitan Magazine, Agron said that when her father was detected with multiple sclerosis, it was the worst phase of her life. Her parents decided to stay apart because of this disease. It was then that Agron at an early age of 15 played the matured role of a woman in keeping his family together in their moments of crisis.
